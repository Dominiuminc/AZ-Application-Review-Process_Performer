Examples of Each Category:

-ICW (Income Calculation Worksheet): A multi-page document that is always titled "Income Calculation Worksheet". It details income from household members. A few datils about the Income Calculation Worksheet is that the first page contains three sections: Household Members, Income Calculations, Asset Calculations. The second page totals both credits income from assets and annual income.

-Application Summary: A three page document with each page number listed on the top right of the form as "1 of 3" or "2 of 3". Page 1 has a header for Application for Rental Housing. Page 2 is the application summary table detailing the household composition and page 3 has the member signature fields and details the penalties for misuing the form.
 
-Asset Self Certification Form: There are two pages, one is labeled Asset Self- Certification and the next is labeled Asset Self-Certification Worksheet. The first page is the Asset Self Certification Form and it contains three parts: Part one label Assets Disposed of for less than fair market value. It includes questions about assets, date of disposal, and FMV. Part two labeled Federal Tax Return or Refundable Tax Credit includes questions on amount of return/ tax credit. Part three is labeled Non Necessary Personal Property and includes questions on Type of Asset, Cash Value, Annual Income.The second page of the form contains two parts: Part one asks questions regarding Determination of Total Net Family Assets  if the familt only has NNPP and No Real Property. Part two asks questions regarding Determination of Total Net Family Assets if the family has both NNPP and Real Property.

-AZ Income Limit Table: A landscape document usually rotated 90 degrees from AZ housing titled "Arizona Income Housing TAX Credit Program - Imputed Incomes/Allowable Rents".

-Income & Asset Questionnaire: A 4 page document with the page number listed on the top right of the form as "1 of 4", "2 of 4", etc. The first page is titled "Income and & Asset Questionnaire" with an income checklist table. The first page contains questions under the Income Checklist section. The section includes questions regarding Employment Wages, Self Employment, Public Assistance, Millitary Wages, etc. The second page contains a table titled "Income Sources". The table contains information regarding Member Name, Income Type, and Contact Information. The third page is a series of check boxes titled "Asset Checklist" and contains information regarding: Checking Account, Savings Account, Life Insurance Policy, Internet Based Assets, etc. The fourth page is a table titled "Asset Sources" and contains information about: Member Name, Asset Type, Asset Source, Annual Income from Asset, Jointly Owned, etc.

-Member Information Document Form: A 1 page document titled "Member Information Document". The document has two section. The first top section of the document contains member information: Full Legal Name, Last Name, Gender, State Issued, etc. The second bottom section is labeled Part A and Part B. Part A is optional to household members who are children, foster adults, or live-in aids. Part B contains information for members that are under 18 years old and not emancipated.

-Screening Documents:There are at least two pages, the first one begins with the label "Screening Report" and will be in english. At the bottom of the first page will be a pagination with numbers 1 through N, where N is the number of screening document pages. Mark N sequential pages begining with this one as screening documents. They should all have the same continuing pagination. This does not include ICRAA disclosures. The first page has a section called Property Screening Result where it contains Application Result. The first page also has a section called Applicant Information, Summary, Experian Credit Report, and Premium National Criminals Records Search. The second page is Powered by Experian RentBureau, Property Acknowledgment, and Additional Information for the Applicant. The third page contains information from Screening Works Pro.

-Student Certification Form: A one page document titled "Student Certification" with a sub header "(For use with the LIHTC Program)". Note this is not the "Annual Student Certification". The document has two parts: Part A and Part B. Part A asks questions about not being a student, being a part-time student, or being a full-time student. Part B asks questions about joint tax return, recieving assitance, and about TANF. At the bottom of the page, it contains signature fields.

-VAWA Acknowledgement of Receipt: This document contains 7 pages with information detailing the program. We are only interested in classifying the last page, which is titled "VAWA Acknowledgement of Receipt" and contains a table with 9 possible Applicant/Resident Signatures. The other pages that do not contain signature fields are to be placed in "Other". 

-Certification of Zero Income Form: A two page document with with the page number listed on the top right of the form as "1 of 2" or "2 of 2". Both the first and second page are titled "Certification of Zero Income", where the first page contains the "Section A" table which asks questions about wages, social security, unemployment, public assitance, child wages, etc. The second contains "Section B" table called Certification of Zero Income that asks questions about Expense Type, Monthly Amount, Source of Payment, N/A.

-Self Certification of Non-Employment Form: A single paged document titled "Self Certification of Non-Employment". The page contains two sections. The first sections asks "Are you currently employed?". The second section asks "Are you expecting to become employed in the next 12 months?".

-Affidavit of Student Financial Assistance: A 2 page document with page numbering at the bottom right of the form. Both pages are titled "Affidavit of Student Financial Assistance" where the first page contains Page contains parts I and II. Part I is called Amounts Recieved Under Section 479B Under of the Higher Education Act (HEA). Part II is called Amount Recieved as Other Student Financial Assistance. The second page contains Parts III and IV. Part III is labeled Other Monenetary Contributions. Part IV is labeled Cover Costs.

-Child Support Self-Certification: One page titled "Child Support Self-Certification". The page has three boxes. Box one asks to "Please list the names of child(ren) this supports for:". -Affidavit of Paternity: One page titled "Affidavit of Paternity". The page contains information regarding Appt #, Resident Name, and Date. May not be present.

-Dominium Release of Information: Single page titled "Release of Information" with Dominium logo up above. One per adult. The top of the page asks "Community You Are Applying For:" and the bottom of the page asks for a signature, Social Security Number, Date of Birth, and Date.

-Emergency Contact Form: Single page titled "Emergency Contact Form". The page starts of by asking for "Property Name:" then has Instructions, Name of Emergency Contact Person or Organization, Reason For Contact, and Confidentiality Statement.
