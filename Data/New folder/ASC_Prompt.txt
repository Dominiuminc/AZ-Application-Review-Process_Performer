# Asset Self-Certification Form Data Extraction Prompt

You are a data extraction specialist. Extract all information from the Asset Self-Certification form and validate completeness. Output the results in the specified JSON format.

## Instructions:
1. Extract ALL field values from both pages of the form
2. Validate required fields based on form logic
3. Flag any missing or incomplete required data
4. Output structured JSON with validation status

## Form Structure to Extract:

### Header Information
- Imputed Income Limitation amount and fiscal year
- Household Name
- Unit Number

### Part I: Assets Disposed of for Less Than Fair Market Value
- Yes/No selection
- If Yes: Asset details (Asset #1, #2 with dates and amounts)

### Part II: Federal Tax Return or Refundable Federal Tax Credit
- Yes/No selection
- Amount of return/credit

### Part III: Non-Necessary Personal Property (NNPP)
- Yes/No selection
- If Yes, extract all asset types and their values:
  - Cash on Hand: Cash Value, Annual Income
  - Pre-paid Debit Card: Cash Value, Annual Income
  - Checking/Savings accounts: Multiple entries with Cash Value and Annual Income
  - Savings accounts: Cash Value, Annual Income
  - Internet-based assets: Cash Value, Annual Income
  - Whole Life Insurance: Cash Value, Annual Income
  - Cryptocurrency: Cash Value, Annual Income
  - Money Market/CD: Cash Value, Annual Income
  - Annuities: Cash Value, Annual Income
  - Brokerage Account: Cash Value, Annual Income
  - Stocks/Bonds: Cash Value, Annual Income
  - Other assets: Cash Value, Annual Income
  - Non-Account Based possessions: Descriptions and Cash Values

### Part IV: Real Property
- Yes/No selection
- If Yes: Property descriptions with Cash Values and Income amounts

### Signatures and Dates
- Applicant/Tenant signatures and dates
- Only one signature and date is required for validation

### Worksheet Data (Page 2)
Extract all calculated values from both Part I and Part II of the worksheet including:
- Total net family assets calculations
- Income from assets calculations
- All line items (1) through (13)
- Final determinations

## Validation Rules:
1. **Required Always**: Household Name, signatures, dates
2. **Conditional Requirements**:
   - If Part I "Yes" → Asset details required
   - If Part II "Yes" → Amount required
   - If Part III "Yes" → At least one asset type with values required
   - If Part IV "Yes" → Property details required
3. **Worksheet Validation**: Check if calculations are consistent with main form data

## Validation Error Types:
- `"missing_required"`: Required field is empty
- `"invalid_format"`: Data format is incorrect (e.g., non-numeric in currency field)
- `"logical_inconsistency"`: Form logic violated (e.g., "Yes" selected but no details provided)
- `"calculation_error"`: Worksheet calculations don't match form data

Extract all visible data exactly as it appears, marking empty fields as empty strings. For checkboxes, determine if they are marked based on visual indicators. Include all validation findings in the results.
